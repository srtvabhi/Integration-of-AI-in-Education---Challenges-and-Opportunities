# 📝 LAB 3 — AI for Assessment Creation

## 🎯 Objective

In this lab, participants will learn how to use AI tools to design effective assessments for higher education.

By the end of this lab, participants will be able to create:

- ✅ Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
- 📚 Descriptive Exam Questions
- 📊 Assessment Rubrics
- 🏆 Bloom’s Taxonomy-Based Question Banks
- 💻 Practical Lab Assessments
- 🛡️ AI-Resistant Assessment Strategies
- 🎯 Quiz Questions for Classroom Teaching

---

# 🚀 Why Use AI for Assessment Creation?

Traditional assessment design often requires significant time and effort.

### ⏳ Traditional Method

- Manual question creation
- Rubric design
- Difficulty-level balancing
- Outcome mapping
- Assessment validation

**Estimated Time:** 2–4 Hours

### ⚡ AI-Assisted Method

- Instant question generation
- Automatic Bloom’s Taxonomy alignment
- Quick rubric creation
- Diverse question formats
- Ready-to-use assessment materials

**Estimated Time:** 2–10 Minutes


---

## 📌 Expected Output

- Instant questioning
- Follow-up discussion

### 📚 Real-World Case Study

- Analyze an actual marketing campaign
- Recommend improvements

### 🪞 Reflective Questions

### 🤝 Collaborative Tasks

- Team-based campaign planning
- Group discussions
- Strategy formulation

### 🎯 Presentation-Based Evaluation

- Campaign presentation

## 🎓 Benefits of AI-Resistant Assessments

- Improves communication skills
- Enhances problem-solving abilities
- Reduces blind AI dependency
